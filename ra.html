About this Charter: This Charter sets out the rights of investors and obligations of Research Analysts, grievance redressal mechanism, and timelines.

1. Roles and responsibilities of Research Analysts

  • Provide unbiased and diligent research, with adequate disclosures.
  • Avoid conflict of interest; maintain records as required.

2. What investors can expect

  • Transparent disclosures, risk statements, and clear communication.
  • Accessible grievance channels and time‑bound resolution.

3. Grievance redressal

Contact us first using the details in the Grievance section below. If unresolved, lodge a complaint on SCORES 2.0.

Accessibility Statement

We aim to comply with WCAG 2.1 Level AA and applicable Indian requirements (GIGW and the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 and rules thereunder). Annual accessibility audits are conducted by IAAP‑certified professionals, including usability testing by persons with disabilities; remediation is implemented based on findings . Public documents are provided in accessible formats . For IAs/RAs, compliance filings are made with BSE Ltd per SEBI directions .
